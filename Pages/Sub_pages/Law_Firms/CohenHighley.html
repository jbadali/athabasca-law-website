Cohen Highley LLP:

Description: Cohen Highley LLP provides professional legal services on an agency basis for corporate counsel, law firms and sole practitioners. Lawyers who are located in municipalities or jurisdictions outside London, Ontario know the agency retainer is more cost-effective for their clients than travelling to London themselves to provide legal services. The agency retainer is also useful for the practitioner/principal who does not have the expertise in a particular area of law. With law practices covering all types of family issues, corporate-commercial matters, real estate transactions, wills and estates planning, administrative law issues, and commercial and civil litigation, our legal professionals can provide a broad range of legal services which you may require to be performed in London or in the London region. If your law firm or corporation requires legal services rendered in a timely manner at reasonable rates, or you would like further information, please contact Paul Vogel by e-mail at vogel@cohenhighley.com or by phone at 519-672-9330.
