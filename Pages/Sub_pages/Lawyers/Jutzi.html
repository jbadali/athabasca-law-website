Lawyer Profile: Thomas (Tom) Jutzi

Tel: (519) 884-2620

Email: tomj@dsjnlaw.com

Tom has broad experience in Corporate and Commercial law transactions, Wills, Estates, Powers of Attorney, Charities and Not-for-Profit Corporations and Real Estate law. The practice of law primarily involves problem-solving, affording the opportunity to apply rules, past experience and acquired insight and common sense to assist clients. Tom endeavours to bring clarity to matters and to keep clients from becoming entangled in disputes. His goal is to combine high quality work with progressive values. Tom is grateful for the many opportunities he has had to complete complex negotiations, close deals and work with clients to help them plan their future. These give a lawyer the experience and confidence to analyze a vast array of legal issues.
