Lawyer Profile: Bob McMaster

Email: bob@johnsonmcmaster.com

Bob has been practicing law since 2004.

Bob started his legal career at a large Toronto law firm before returning to his home town of Cambridge, Ontario. Initially, he practiced personal injury and disability law, representing clients in insurance matters before the Superior Court of Justice, as well as various administrative tribunals including the Criminal Injuries Compensation Board, the Canada Pension Plan Review Tribunal, and the Pension Appeals Board.

In 2008, Bob focused his practice on real estate, wills, and estates. Since then, he has acted on thousands of real estate transactions for buyers, sellers, and lenders all over Southwestern Ontario including the Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, Guelph, Milton, and Brantford areas.

Bob strives to make all his clients feel comfortable. He is well-known in the real estate community as a conscientious advocate with a good sense of humour.
