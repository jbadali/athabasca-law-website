Lawyer Profile: Kristina Tesser

Email: kristina@johnsonmcmaster.com

Kristina Tesser is an associate at Johnson McMaster practicing in the areas of real estate, wills and estates, and family law.

Kristina’s real estate practice focuses on residential purchases and sales, mortgages, re-financing, and transfers of title. She particularly enjoys helping new home buyers navigate the legal issues of their first home purchase.

Kristina’s background as a licensed funeral director provides valuable insight when assisting clients with their wills and powers of attorney, and other estate planning issues. Having seen the first hand consequences of what happens when a person dies without leaving a will, Kristina understands the importance of providing guidance and protection for your loved ones through an effective and clearly written last testament.

Kristina’s family practice focuses on assisting clients with co-habitation agreements (“pre-nups”) and separation agreements. Kristina’s former experience as a litigator in and out of the courtroom is invaluable when assisting clients through a breakdown in their marriage or common-law union. Kristina is an experienced and effective negotiator, and has helped many clients ease the tensions that arise in divorce and separation. She achieves this through practical negotiation, and drafting comprehensive and fair separation agreements that settle all issues, including child custody and access, support, and division of property. Once a strong separation agreement is in place, Kristina can assist you with completing your divorce.

Kristina received her honours bachelor specialist degree in History and Political Science with high distinction from the University of Toronto at Mississauga. Kristina then studied law at the University of Toronto where she received her Juris Doctor degree in 2012. She was called to the Ontario Bar in 2013. While in law school, Kristina worked at Downtown Legal Services as a department leader in Family Law. She articled in the Waterloo office of a national law firm and practiced family law litigation for the same law firm for several years. Kristina joined Johnson McMaster in late 2014.
