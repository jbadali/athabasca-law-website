Lawyer Profile: Michael Miller

  • Real Estate
  • Commercial Real Estate
  • Corporate Law

    Michael has been practicing law with our firm since his call to the Bar in 2001. Mike completed law school at Osgoode Hall Law School and fulfilled his articling year in Toronto at which time he returned home to practice with our firm.

    Mike has extensive experience in all areas of Real Estate including residential purchases and sales, refinance transactions as well as Commercial Real Estate including leases, commercial mortgages and commercial purchases and sales.

    Mike also practices in the area of Corporate Law including Incorporations, the purchase or sale of a business, partnership and shareholder agreements, and Professional Incorporations.

    Mike enjoys skiing, golf and playing hockey. He is actively involved in the community, serving on the Board of KIDS CAN PLAY, a charitable organization that raises funds to support families who can’t afford to enroll their children in cultural and recreational activities.
