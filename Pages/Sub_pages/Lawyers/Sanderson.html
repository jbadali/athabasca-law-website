Lawyer Profile: Thomas E. Sanderson

Tel: (519) 886-3340 ext. 284

Email: tsanderson@kwlaw.net

Expertise:

  • Commercial Litigation
  • Employment Law
  • Construction Law
  • Municipal & Planning Law

Thomas articled with the firm, and joined as an associate upon his 2014 call to the bar. Originally from Flamborough, Ontario, he attended the University of Toronto for his Honours Bachelor of Arts (2010) and Juris Doctor (2013). He was awarded the Thomas J. Lang, Thomas Goudge, John MacDonald, and George Edison Scholarships in undergraduate philosophy; and the Blakes Scholarship at law school.

While in school, Thomas worked as a teaching assistant with U of T’s Criminology Program, and served as a Senior Editor of the U of T Faculty of Law Review. He also enjoyed volunteer research and casework positions with the Canadian Civil Liberties Association, the ADR Institute of Canada, and the David Asper Centre for Constitutional Rights.
