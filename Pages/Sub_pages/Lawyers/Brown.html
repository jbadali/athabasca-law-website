Lawyer Profile: Reginald (Reg) Brown

Reginald "Reg" Brown

Tel: (519) 884-2620

Email: reginaldb@dsjnlaw.com

Reg practices in the areas of real estate and corporate and commercial law.

With a growing focus on residential real estate, he is able to assist vendors and purchasers with the drafting and preparation of purchase and sale agreements, as well as with the completion of other documentation in connection with their real estate transactions. Reg is also able to assist with the preparation and submission of various property related applications to local municipalities, including land severance and minor variance applications.

Having focused strongly on business law, both as a student and as a burgeoning lawyer, Reg is able to assist with incorporations, amalgamations, and company reorganizations, and he is further able to provide effective services in a wide variety of commercial matters relating to corporate governance, asset and share transactions as well as commercial real estate.

Reg enjoys the challenges that real estate, corporate and commercial law present, and he looks forward to settling into these roles as his practice continues to evolve.
