Lawyer Profile: Daniel W. Veinot

Tel: (519) 886-3340 ext. 282

Email: dveinot@kwlaw.net

Expertise:

  • Commercial Litigation
  • Municipal & Planning Law
  • Employment Law
  • Family Law
  • Bankruptcy, Insolvency and Restructuring

Originally from New Germany, Nova Scotia, Daniel articled with the firm and joined the firm as an associate upon his call to the bar in 2008.

Daniel focuses his practice on civil litigation with an emphasis on construction, bankruptcy and municipal law.

A graduate of the French common law program of the University of Ottawa, Daniel practices in both official languages. During his studies, Daniel worked for the Public Service Labour Relations Board and volunteered his time with Pro Bono Students Canada. Daniel also holds a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from the Université Sainte-Anne in Pointe-de-l’Église, Nova Scotia.
