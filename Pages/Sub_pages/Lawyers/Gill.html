Lawyer Profile: Ramanjit K. Gill

Tel: (519) 884-2620

Email: ramang@dsjnlaw.com

Ramanjit ("Raman") completed her Bachelor of Arts from York University and graduated from the University of Leicester’s Faculty of Law (UK) in 2013. Raman completed her articles in Civil and Family Litigation North York, Toronto and upon completion of her Articles of Clerkship was admitted to the Law Society of Upper Canada in 2015.

Raman joins our firm after practicing Family Law exclusively with a reputable firm in Windsor, Ontario where she gained invaluable experience in family law practice and procedure. She has a proven track record of working cooperatively to achieve the best results for her clients through close interaction with those clients to identify, assess and address their unique concerns, as well as manage their expectations throughout the legal process.

Raman’s passion for helping people understand and deal with their legal problems, drives her ambition, allowing her to obtain the best results for her clients. Raman’s primary focus is on the resolution of Civil and Family disputes.
