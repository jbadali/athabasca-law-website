Lawyer Profile: David M. Steele

Tel: (519) 886-3340

Email: dms@kwlaw.net

Expertise:

  • Commercial Litigation
  • Construction Law
  • Employment Law
  • Bankruptcy, Insolvency and Restructuring

David was called to the Bar in Ontario in 1991 and has been at the firm since 1993. After graduating from the University of Toronto with a degree in engineering, he completed the joint LL.B./M.B.A. program at McGill University. His practice concentrates predominately on business clientele including a major Canadian insurance company dealing with such issues as professional negligence, breach of contract, employment law and debtor-creditor disputes.
